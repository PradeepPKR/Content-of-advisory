Youth United Council of India is a youth-run organization with a central team of passionate and zealous young individuals driven to bring about change. YUCI acts as a unique platform for youth that aids and empowers them through various programs and opportunities. YUCI aims to tackle all issues that hamper the growth and development of young individuals by bringing a wholesome solution. The youth of this council take action to bring change and work towards youth empowerment with their call to action. YUCI is dedicated to strengthen the youth community and help them achieve success in sustainable development.
